About Us

Telangana Academy for Skill and Knowledge TASK is a not-for-profit organization created by the Government of Telangana to bring synergy among institutions of Government, Industry & Academia to offer quality human resources and services to the industry. Established in 2004 as IEG/ JKC and renamed as TASK in 2014.
